Our Project

The product that we are making will be able to detect a celebrity’s face and tell the user who the celebrity is with a high rate of accuracy. For this project, we decided to do marvel actors and had a total of seven classes. We decided to this project because sometimes people will come across pictures of celebrities, but cannot think of the celebrity’s name on top of their head. Instead of not being able to sleep with the question of who this celebrity is, they can use our website.
